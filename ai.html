What the AI Layer Does

The AI layer is the reasoning engine behind your onboarding system. It interprets your knowledge hub , understands your onboarding processes, and provides real-time support to your team and clients.

It is not a chatbot bolted onto your documentation — it is an embedded intelligence layer that works across your entire onboarding workflow.

  • Delivers instant, context-aware answers — internal & external
  • Guides users through onboarding steps
  • Explains “how” and “why,” not just “what”
  • Reduces support load on your team
  • Accelerates new hire ramp-up
  • Improves as new data enters the system

The AI Agents Inside Your Onboarding Engine

Internal Support Agent

Your team’s always-on expert. Answers internal questions instantly: process steps, decisions, client scenarios, exceptions, workflows, documentation locations—anything.

Replaces repeated internal questions.

Client-Facing Agent

A guided, professional experience for your clients. Helps them understand their onboarding steps, timelines, requirements, and next actions.

Clients feel supported and in control.

Product / Platform Agent

An AI layer trained on your product documentation, workflows, and configuration rules. Helps users navigate features and reduces dependency on your support team.

Ramps up new users without training calls.

How the AI Layer Learns

Unlike traditional AI chatbots that rely on unstructured files, the PDL Engine organizes your onboarding content into a clean, predictable structure that AI can interpret with accuracy.

1. Knowledge Hub

Data vault architecture designed for AI readability — steps, decisions, sequences, FAQs, templates, and logic.

2. Automated Ingestion Pipelines

New or updated onboarding content flows directly into the AI layer without manual work.

3. Continuous Reinforcement

Each onboarding cycle enriches the system — processes improve, AI improves, onboarding improves.
