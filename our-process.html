Plan → Do → Launch

Our End-to-End Onboarding System Build

A complete process that aligns your people, your systems, and your AI—so onboarding becomes predictable, scalable, and simple.

Step 1

Discovery & Gap Analysis

We begin by understanding how onboarding works today—and where it breaks.

  • Map your “as-is” onboarding workflow
  • Interview sales, onboarding, and support teams
  • Identify confusion, delays, and bottlenecks
  • Collect and audit all current onboarding materials
Step 2

Design the Ideal Onboarding System

A clear, simple blueprint for how onboarding should work moving forward.

  • Define onboarding phases & success criteria
  • Design new handoffs, steps, and owners
  • Eliminate inconsistencies and guesswork
  • Create your complete onboarding playbook
Step 3

Build Your Knowledge Engine (Obsidian)

Your single source of truth—used by both your team and your AI agent.

  • Create structured, organized Obsidian vault
  • Convert onboarding docs into clean .md files
  • Add YAML metadata & cross-linking
  • Create step-by-step guides, templates, and SOPs
Step 4

Make Everything AI Ready

We restructure your onboarding content for reliable AI responses.

  • Atomize documents into smaller, precise notes
  • Create summaries and context for AI
  • Add owner, step, and dependency metadata
  • Run embedding tests for accuracy
Step 5

Back-End Setup (Supabase)

Supabase becomes the back-end infrastructure for your client portal.

  • Database + schema setup for clients & tasks
  • Authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Secure data access using Row Level Security
  • Create APIs for your portal to use
Step 6

Build the Client Portal (Next.js)

A modern, responsive onboarding portal for both clients and internal teams.

  • Client dashboards & onboarding views
  • Task lists, timelines & status indicators
  • Embedded guides & training from Obsidian
  • Internal “admin mode” for your team
Step 7

Integrate the AI Onboarding Agent

Your agent becomes a support partner for clients and your internal team.

  • Create embeddings from your Obsidian vault
  • Connect portal to OpenAI Assistants API
  • Enable “What’s next?” & “How do I…?” Q&A
  • AI-guided tasks & context-aware answers
Step 8

Launch, Training & Improvement

We deploy the system, support your team, and refine based on real usage.

  • Team training on the new onboarding process
  • Training on using the AI agent
  • Monitor early client onboarding sessions
  • Quarterly updates & optimization cycles
