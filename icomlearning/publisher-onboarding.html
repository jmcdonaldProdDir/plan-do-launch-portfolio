Compliance Platform Release Notes for August 2025

Publisher Onboarding

A complete plan, task list, and the resources and processes required to have a successful project.

PREPARATION TASKS

  • Project kick off meeting
  • Collect existing rollover forms and examples and send them to your Onboarding Specialist
  • Watch the RolloverAnalyzer videos for training and configurations
  • Review Gap Analysis with your Onboarding Specialist

PUBLISHER CONFIGURATIONS

  • Configure Client Info fields
  • Configure Account Types
  • Configure Service and Fit questions
  • Configure Recommendation templates
  • Validation and sign off on configurations

Rollout preparation

  • Review Pilot Project plan
  • Define the Pilot Group
  • Send an Introduction to RolloverAnalyzer (2 minute video)
  • Draft welcome email
  • Branding of Training Material and QuickStart Guides
  • Review support plan
  • Assign support resources

Rollout to advisors

Phase 1 - Pilot Project
  • Send welcome letter to the Pilot Group
  • Gather feedback and update configurations
Phase 2 - Company Wide Rollout
  • Prepare for heavy support for the first two days by having all your support resources available and making InvestorCOM aware of your rollout dates.
  • Work with the InvestorCOM Customer Success team to understand adoption issues and increase user uptake.
