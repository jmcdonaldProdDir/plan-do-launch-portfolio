PeerCompare Onboarding

A complete plan, task list, and the resources and processes required to have a successful project.

ONBOARDING TASKS

  • Project kickoff meeting
  • Watch video "A Typical Advisor Needs to Consider One Million Data Points - Every Single Day"
  • Download, and complete the Request for billing information form
  • Download and complete the Client Users Template (Excel file)
  • Watch the PeerCompare videos for training and configurations
  • Setup sender authentication for SendGrid - MORE INFO AT SENDGRID 

configuration tasks

  • Configure securities
  • Configure ShelfMonitor, and PeerCompare settings for matching, identification and measurement
  • Upload the Product Shelf
  • Set PeerCompare options
  • Configuration Recommendation template
  • Configuration Disclosure template
  • Configure ShelfMonitor alerts
  • Validation and sign off on configurations

Rollout preparation

  • Review pilot project plan
  • Define the pilot group
  • Send the introduction to PeerCompare video
  • Draft the welcome email
  • Branding of training material and QuickStart guide
  • Draft a support plan
  • Assign support resources

Rollout to advisors

  • Phase 1 - Pilot Project
  • Send welcome letter to the Pilot Group
  • Start the pilot
  • End the pilot
  • Gather feedback and update configurations
  • Phase 2 - Company Wide Rollout
  • Prepare for heavy support for the first two days by having all your support resources available and making InvestorCOM aware of your rollout dates.
  • Work with the InvestorCOM Customer Success team to understand adoption issues and increase user uptake.
