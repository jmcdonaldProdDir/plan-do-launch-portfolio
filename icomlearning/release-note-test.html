Release 23.2 - Compliance Platform Enhancements/Fixes

Allow Variable Annuities to be Filtered by VA Product Shelf

Overview
Clients using variable annuities have limited peer-matching criteria to use and, as a result, get peer group listings in the range of 250 pages. The goal of this enhancement is to shorten that list to something reasonable - 2 pages at most. Please note that a recent change to the Peer Group listing page did provide the ability to reduce the size of the list. However, that same list would be part of the peer group comparison in PeerCompare. 

With this enhancement, clients may now upload a product shelf that includes variable annuities and use that shelf data to filter their data. 

Enhancement
  1. Include variable annuities on the product shelf.
  2. Use the securities on the product shelf to filter the VAs, to get smaller peer group listings, and peer matching groups.
Process
  1. Upload the product shelf, and include variable annuities (US, that is, Morningstar codes).  See sample below.
  2. Configure PeerCompare to use the product shelf for comparisons instead of the universe. 

Client Impact
  • The client will see fewer peers in matching results in both PeerCompare and ShelfAnalyzer.
  • The client will have fewer pages of peer group listings in the recommendation/disclosure documents.
  • The client will be made aware of any changes, additions and deletions to the VA product shelf.
