About John McDonald

The person behind Plan → Do → Launch

John McDonald is the founder of Plan → Do → Launch, a consultancy focused on helping growing SaaS and compliance-driven organizations fix the one thing that quietly slows everything down: onboarding.

For more than two decades, John has led product, operations, and client-facing teams through complex onboarding, implementation, and regulatory workflows. Before creating Plan → Do → Launch, he spent years inside organizations where onboarding lived in people’s heads, processes changed weekly, and customers waited for clarity that never quite arrived.

Today, John works with companies who are winning business but struggling to bring new clients or staff on board smoothly. Through a clear, human-first approach, he helps teams turn scattered knowledge, inconsistent workflows, and “hero-based” onboarding into simple, repeatable systems that scale.

John’s background blends product leadership, onboarding design, regulatory expertise, and AI-ready content strategy — giving him a unique ability to take something complex, see the underlying structure, and rebuild it in a way that feels obvious in hindsight.

At his core, John believes that great onboarding isn’t just a process — it’s one of the strongest competitive advantages a business can build.

He lives in London, Ontario and works with clients across Canada and the United States.
