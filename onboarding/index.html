AI in this workspace

How AI shows up in your onboarding system

We don’t bolt on a chatbot and call it innovation. In this workspace, AI shows up as a set of focused copilots that sit on top of your documented process and content — always human-first, always grounded in your own onboarding materials.

Human-first · AI-enabled · Guardrails built in

Client Onboarding Copilot

For your customers

An embedded AI assistant in your onboarding portal that answers “How do I…?” questions using your guides, FAQs, and policies. It points clients to the right step, link, or checklist — so your team gets fewer repetitive tickets.

  • Trained on your onboarding docs, not generic internet data
  • Available 24/7 inside the portal
  • Can suggest next steps and link to relevant resources

Delivery Copilot

For your internal team

A private copilot for your onboarding, CS, and implementation teams. It surfaces SOPs, edge cases, and checklists so new hires ramp faster and experts aren’t interrupted for the same questions over and over.

  • Grounded in your playbooks, SOPs, and checklists
  • Helps answer “What’s our process if…?” in plain language
  • Supports consistent delivery across regions and roles

Summaries & Insights

For leaders and owners

AI-assisted summaries of discovery calls, workshops, and status updates. We use this to highlight risks, blockers, and wins — then capture them here in the workspace so everyone stays aligned.

  • Call and workshop summaries in plain English
  • Lists of risks, decisions, and follow-ups
  • Input into status reports and onboarding metrics
In early engagements, we often run these copilots “behind the scenes” for you. As your team gets comfortable, we can embed them directly into your onboarding portal and internal tools.
Deep Engagement Option

Need Hands-On Help Building It All?

Some teams don’t just need an onboarding system — they need the assets that bring it to life. When your onboarding or customer success team is at capacity, I embed as a fractional onboarding partner to design and build everything with you, including AI-ready content and flows.

High-touch · High-leverage · Time-bound

Embedded Onboarding Partner (3 Months)

For teams that need an MVP onboarding kit

A focused, 12–14 week engagement where I partner with your onboarding or CS lead to create the core assets your team needs to onboard clients consistently.

  • “Getting Started” & “Go Live” user guides
  • Short, branded video training modules
  • Core FAQ library for internal and external use
  • Onboarding playbooks and checklists for your team
  • Copy for key onboarding emails and in-app messages

Typical investment: starting around $25K–$30K USD (~$34K–$40K CAD) for the 3-month sprint.

Embedded Onboarding Residency (6 Months)

For organizations ready to scale onboarding

A deeper, 24–26 week residency where I act as a fractional onboarding enablement lead — designing, building, and refining a full onboarding ecosystem alongside your team.

  • Everything in the 3-month Partner engagement
  • Structured knowledge base or help centre buildout
  • Internal enablement materials for new hires
  • Onboarding metrics & reporting views
  • Designing and training an AI “Onboarding Copilot” on your onboarding content

Typical investment: starting around $50K–$70K USD (~$67K–$95K CAD) for the 6-month residency.

Is an Embedded Partnership Right for You?

Good fit if
  • You have strong product–market fit but inconsistent onboarding
  • Your best people are too busy to document what they know
  • You want strategic thinking and hands-on execution in one person
  • You’d like to avoid hiring a full-time director-level role right now

The result is simple: when we’re done, you’ll have a complete onboarding system, a library of assets, and the internal playbooks your team can run without me.
