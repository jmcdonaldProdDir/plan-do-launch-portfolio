InsightCompare Onboarding

A complete plan, task list, and the resources and processes required to deliver a successful implementation.

Onboarding Tasks

  • Project kickoff meeting
  • Watch video: "A Typical Advisor Reviews Thousands of Data Points Every Day"
  • Download and complete the Request for Billing Information form
  • Download and complete the Client Users Template (Excel file)
  • Watch the InsightCompare videos for training and configurations
  • Set up sender authentication with your email provider – More info at SendGrid

Configuration Tasks

  • Configure securities / product universe
  • Configure ProductWatch and InsightCompare settings for matching, identification and measurement
  • Upload the product inventory (Product Shelf)
  • Set InsightCompare options
  • Configure Recommendation template
  • Configure Disclosure template
  • Configure ProductWatch alerts
  • Validation and sign off on configurations

Rollout Preparation

  • Review pilot project plan
  • Define the pilot group
  • Send the introduction to InsightCompare video
  • Draft the welcome email
  • Brand training material and QuickStart Guide
  • Draft a support plan
  • Assign support resources

Rollout to Advisors

  • Phase 1 - Pilot Project
  • Send welcome letter to the pilot group
  • Start the pilot
  • End the pilot
  • Gather feedback and update configurations
  • Phase 2 - Company Wide Rollout
  • Prepare for heavy support for the first two days by having all support resources available and making the Customer Success team aware of your rollout dates.
  • Work with the Customer Success team to understand adoption issues and increase user uptake.
